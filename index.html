my philosophies:
  • its never too late to start now, but it might be too late in the future
    • dont use "its never too late to start" as an excuse for procrastination
    • avoid regret by taking action
    • time is always ticking; use it wisely
  • put your eggs in one basket
    • sometimes you only have one egg and basket
    • pressure drives performance, creates strength and refines character
    • singular focus allows for compounding effort; you cant reach the top without commitment
  • most limits are self imposed
    • people are their own worst critics
    • human brains are hardwired to seek comfort and avoid risk
    • its only impossible if you believe it
  • go fast
    • momentum matters
    • speed forces clarity
    • slow is fear and hesitation disguised
    • the faster you go, the faster you grow
  • dont think, just do
    • analysis paralysis is real
    • you learn more from doing than thinking
    • trust your intuition; its usually right
  • hard work never goes away
    • your efforts are never wasted but stored
    • you become a person that can do hard things
    • The return comes later and often from places you never expected.
  • be an egoist
    • dont confuse confidence with competence
    • your actions should embody your ego; what you do everyday should move you closer to your goals
    • never settle for less
    • let your ego be the engine
  • its okay to relax and have fun
    • enjoy life!
